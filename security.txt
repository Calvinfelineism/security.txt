# Disclosure Policy:
# We encourage the responsible disclosure of security vulnerabilities. If you discover a vulnerability, please report it promptly. We aim to acknowledge receipt of reports within 72 hours and will provide a status update within 5 business days.
# Please refrain from publicly disclosing any vulnerabilities until we have had an opportunity to investigate and address the issue.

# Scope of Reporting:
# This policy applies to all services, systems, and applications directly managed and operated by calvinfelineism.org.

# Safe Harbor:
# Activities conducted in a manner consistent with this policy will be considered authorized conduct, and we will not initiate legal action against security researchers who engage in good faith discovery and reporting.
